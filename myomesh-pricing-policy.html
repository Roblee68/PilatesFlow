Subscription Plans

MyoMesh offers three monthly subscription tiers. Every plan includes the full suite of features — scheduling, payments, SOAP notes, analytics, integrations, and more. Plans differ only by the number of staff members allowed (including the account owner).

Plan Staff Limit Monthly Price (CAD)
MyoGrow Up to 3 staff $55

All Features Included

There are no feature gates between plans. Every MyoMesh subscriber gets access to online booking, Stripe & PayPal payments, QuickBooks sync, Google & Microsoft Calendar integration, custom session pricing, staff expense tracking, SOAP notes, injury tracking, analytics, double-booking protection, and email/calendar reminders.

SMS Add-On Packs

SMS appointment reminders and notifications are available as an optional add-on to any plan. Each pack provides a monthly allotment of outbound text messages. Unused messages do not roll over to the following month.

SMS Pack Changes

Adding or upgrading an SMS pack takes effect immediately and a prorated charge applies for the remainder of the current billing cycle. Removing or downgrading an SMS pack is scheduled to take effect on your next billing date — you'll continue to have access to your current pack for the rest of the month.

3

Free Trial

Every new MyoMesh account starts with a 14-day free trial on any plan. You get full access to all features during your trial — no credit card required to start.

At the end of your trial, your subscription will convert to a paid plan at the monthly rate shown above. If you choose not to continue, you can cancel at any time during the trial with no charge.

Trial Cancellation

If you cancel during your trial, your account will remain accessible until the trial end date. No charge will be made. You'll have 30 days after your trial ends to download your data.

Billing Cycle & Payment

MyoMesh subscriptions are billed monthly on the 1st of each month. Your monthly charge includes your plan fee plus the cost of any active SMS add-on pack.

We accept payment via Stripe (credit and debit cards). You can view your billing details, update your payment method, and review your payment history from the Subscription section in your MyoMesh settings.

Past Due Payments

If a payment fails, your account will be marked as Past Due. We'll retry the charge and notify you by email. Please update your payment method promptly to avoid any interruption to your service.

Upgrading Your Plan

You can upgrade your plan at any time from your settings. Upgrades take effect immediately, giving you instant access to the higher staff limit.

When you upgrade mid-cycle, we calculate a prorated charge for the remaining days in the current month. This is the price difference between your old and new plan, divided by the days in the month, multiplied by the days remaining. This one-time charge is billed immediately. Starting with your next billing date, you'll be charged the full new monthly rate.

Example: Mid-Month Upgrade

You're on MyoGrow ($55) and upgrade to MyoBuild ($85) on January 15. The price difference is $30/month. January has 31 days and 16 remain. Your prorated charge today is $15.48 (= $30 ÷ 31 × 16). On February 1, your regular charge becomes $85/month.

6

Downgrading Your Plan

Downgrades are scheduled for your next billing date. You'll continue to enjoy the benefits of your current plan for the remainder of the billing cycle, and the lower rate will apply starting on the 1st of the following month. No partial refund is issued for the current month.

Before a downgrade can be processed, you must ensure your active staff count does not exceed the staff limit of the plan you're moving to. If you currently have more active staff than the lower plan allows, you'll need to pause or remove staff members before the downgrade can proceed.

Staff Count Check

For example, if you're on MyoPro (16 staff) and want to downgrade to MyoBuild (8 staff), you must have 8 or fewer active staff members before the downgrade can be confirmed.

Staff Limits

Each plan includes a maximum number of active staff members (including the account owner). Staff limits are enforced when inviting new team members or reactivating paused accounts.

Paused staff members do not count toward your active staff limit. If you've reached your plan's limit, you can pause an existing staff member to free up a slot, or upgrade to a higher plan for additional capacity.

Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your MyoMesh subscription at any time from your settings. All paid cancellations are subject to a 30-day notice period.

When you cancel a paid subscription, you are required to pay through the end of the following calendar month. Your account will remain fully accessible until that date, and you'll have an additional 30 days after access ends to download your data.

Example: Cancelling on January 15

January 15 — Cancellation submitted 30-day notice period begins. Your service continues normally.
February 1 — Final payment charged Your last monthly charge is processed. Full access continues through the month.
February 28 — Account access ends Your subscription is fully cancelled. No further charges will be made.
March 30 — Data download deadline You have 30 days after access ends to export your client data, notes, and records.
Trial Accounts

If you cancel during your free trial, cancellation is immediate and no charge is made. You'll retain access until your trial end date and have 30 days after that to download your data.

9

Refund Policy

Monthly subscription fees are non-refundable. Because all plans are billed monthly with no long-term commitment, and every new account begins with a free trial, we do not offer partial or full refunds for subscription charges already processed.

If you believe you've been charged in error, or have exceptional circumstances, please reach out to our support team. We'll review your case individually.

Prorated Charges

Prorated charges from mid-cycle upgrades (plan or SMS) are also non-refundable. When upgrading, you'll see the exact prorated amount before confirming the change.

10

Currency & Tax

Prices shown on our pricing page are in Canadian Dollars (CAD) by default. MyoMesh also supports billing in USD, GBP, AUD, NZD, and EUR. Your billing currency is determined by your account settings and cannot be changed after your first payment is processed.

All prices are exclusive of applicable taxes. Depending on your location, sales tax, VAT, or GST/HST may be added to your invoice. Tax amounts will be clearly shown before payment is processed.

If we make material changes — such as price adjustments or changes to cancellation terms — we'll notify you by email at least 30 days before the changes take effect. Continued use of MyoMesh after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
